Our Story

Driven by Passion, Built on Trust

BRO CARS was founded in 2025 with a single promise: to make the car buying and selling experience honest, easy, and rewarding. What began as a small showroom on Galle Road in Dehiwala has grown into one of UK's most respected automotive dealerships.

We specialise in pre-owned vehicles that are rigorously tested, transparently priced, and backed by our reputation for integrity. Whether you're a first-time buyer or a seasoned motorist, BRO CARS is where great deals and great relationships begin.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission MISSION

To provide UK motorists with access to certified, quality pre-owned vehicles at fair and transparent prices — supported by professional service that makes every transaction smooth, secure, and satisfying. We are committed to building lasting relationships with our customers based on honesty, respect, and a genuine love for automobiles. Every customer who walks through our doors deserves to leave not just with a great car, but with complete peace of mind.

🔭

Our Vision VISION

To become UK's most trusted and innovative automotive dealership — a brand synonymous with quality, integrity, and customer-first values. We envision a future where buying and selling a car is as seamless as it is exciting; where technology and personal service combine to create an unparalleled showroom experience. BRO CARS aspires to set the gold standard for the pre-owned vehicle industry across the island and beyond.

Our Commitment

The BRO CARS Quality Promise

We stand behind every vehicle we sell. Here's exactly what that commitment looks like.

🔍

Rigorous Pre-Sale Inspection

Our certified technicians examine every vehicle across 120 checkpoints — mechanical, cosmetic, electrical, and safety systems — before any car earns a spot in our showroom.
